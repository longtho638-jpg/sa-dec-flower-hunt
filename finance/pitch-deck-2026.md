## 1. The Problem: A $1B Market Stuck in 1990
- **Chaos**: 2,000+ farmers in Sa Dec trade via handshake and Zalo. No contracts, no data.
- **Waste**: 30% of flowers perish due to demand-supply mismatch and poor logistics.
- **Opaque**: Buyers (traders/consumers) have zero price transparency or quality assurance.

## 2. The Solution: "Grab" for Flowers
**Sa Dec Flower Hunt** is the first O2O (Online-to-Offline) platform connecting farmers directly to buyers.
- **For Farmers**: A "Super App" to manage inventory, listing, and payouts (think *Shopify*).
- **For Buyers**: A gamified marketplace to "hunt" the best flowers (think *Pokemon Go* meets *Etsy*).
