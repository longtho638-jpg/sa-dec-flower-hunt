# SADEC.OS Brand Assets

## Logo Usage

The SADEC.OS logo consists of:
- Primary Color: Emerald (#10B981)
- Background: Black (#000000)
- Font: Mono for tech feel

## Brand Colors

- **Emerald 500**: #10B981 (Primary)
- **Stone 950**: #0c0a09 (Background)
- **Amber 500**: #F59E0B (Accent)

## Typography

- **Headlines**: Light weight, clean
- **Body**: System fonts
- **Code/Data**: Monospace

## Usage Guidelines

1. Maintain clear space around the logo
2. Do not distort or recolor
3. Use on dark backgrounds preferred
